Privacy Policy – WiFi Analyzer

Effective Date: March 27, 2026

WiFi Analyzer (“we”, “our”, or “us”), developed by OrbitForge Studios, respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user data.

1. Information We Collect

WiFi Analyzer does not collect, store, or share any personal user data.

However, the app may request the following permissions:

  • Location Permission (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION):
    Required by Android to scan nearby WiFi networks. We do not access or store your actual location.

  • WiFi Permissions (ACCESS_WIFI_STATE, CHANGE_WIFI_STATE):
    Used only to analyze WiFi networks and display relevant information such as signal strength and network details.

2. How We Use Information

All data (such as WiFi scan results) is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to any server.

3. Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell user data
  • Share data with third parties
  • Store personal information

4. Third-Party Services

This app does not use third-party services that collect personal data.
(If you later add ads or analytics, this section must be updated accordingly.)

5. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or stored, there is minimal risk of data exposure.

6. Children’s Privacy

WiFi Analyzer is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
